Career
Guidance

Career guidance, also known as career counseling or career advising, is a process of providing individuals with information, support, and resources to help them make informed decisions about their career and professional development. The goal of career guidance is to assist individuals in exploring and planning their career paths, setting career goals, and overcoming obstacles to achieve those goals.

Career
Change

A career change, also known as a career transition or career shift, refers to the process of moving from one occupation or profession to another. It involves leaving one field or job role and entering a different one, often in a new industry or with significantly different job responsibilities. Career changes can be motivated by various factors, including personal interests, dissatisfaction with one's current career, the desire for new challenges, or changing life circumstances.

Educational
Path

An educational path refers to the journey or sequence of educational experiences and achievements that an individual follows to acquire knowledge, skills, and qualifications in a specific field or discipline. It outlines the steps and milestones one takes from the beginning of their educational journey through various stages of learning and training to achieve their academic and career goals.

Enrollment
Status

Enrollment status refers to an individual's current state or condition in relation to their enrollment in an educational institution or program. It signifies whether a person is actively registered and participating in classes, has temporarily paused their enrollment, or has completed their studies and is no longer enrolled. Enrollment status is a critical element in educational administration and plays a role in determining eligibility for various benefits, financial aid, and access to educational resources.
